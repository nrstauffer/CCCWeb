WHO WE ARE

  • MISSION - Seeking to love God and People
  • VISION - Christ Community Church is committed to growing in the grace and truth of Jesus Christ in order to impact, influence, and transform the larger community and culture for the glory of God. Our task is to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ and to equip every member to serve and obey His commands faithfully.
  • REFORMED CHURCH IN AMERICA - Our congregation is a congregation of the Reformed Church in America. ​We are a member of the Iliana-Florida Classis and of the Synod of Mid-America.
